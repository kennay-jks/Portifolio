Company History

Idrite was founded in 2019 by me, Jack Kennedy Silva. At that time, I already had two years of experience in web design and brand design, but it was only in 2019 that I truly specialized in brand design. That’s what led me to create Idrite a company focused on building visual identities. I faced many challenges along the way and almost gave up, but like any good Brazilian, I pushed through and solved the problems. As a result, in 2024, I was able to stabilize the business and hire my first employee. Now, in 2025, I’ve hired one more and am training two others all to ensure we deliver excellent work to every one of our clients.

Who We Are

We are a company specialized in creating visual identities. This includes logo design, typography, color palettes, letterhead, slide/presentation templates, uniforms, brand manuals, and more. All of our projects are carefully planned to bring the best results for our clients. The visual identity of your brand defines your target audience and the value you can charge for your product or service. Your brand cannot be entrusted to just anyone. If you’re looking for quality service, I would be happy to work with you.
